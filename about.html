ABOUT

Hey there!

I'm a senior product designer designing for a renewable energy future at Arcadia. In a past life, I designed at The Atlantic, coded at Concur, and created graphics for a tea shop. My guiding design philosophy is that technology should be mindfully used to enhance people's lives instead of distract from it.

In my spare time, I read books, take long meandering walks, and make time-consuming desserts. Even in my down time, my preferred mode of being is making and learning, and I do it all with a cup of citron honey tea.

Feel free to contact me. I enjoy getting emails, so more power to you.
