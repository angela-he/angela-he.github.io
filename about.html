ABOUT

Hey there!

After graduating with computer science & visual arts degrees from Emory University, I'm now designing story-telling products at The Atlantic. In a past life, I was a front-end developer in Seattle tech, data viz designer at an edu lab, and graphic designer for an eccentric tea shop.

My philosophy as a designer is that technology should be mindfully used to enhance people's lives instead of distract from it.

In my spare time, I read books, journal, and listen to the 99% Invisible podcast. In other words, my down time still consists of making and learning, and I do it all with a cup of citron honey tea. I'm a firm believer in early mornings, late nights, and being awake in general.

Feel free to contact me with questions, hellos, and life wisdom. I enjoy getting emails, so more power to you.
