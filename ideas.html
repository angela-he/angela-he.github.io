The Atlantic - Ideas Section

Designs for a new section of opinion and analysis-driven pieces

In collaboration with Chris Sullivan, I designed the reading experience for Ideas, The Atlantic's new section for analysis, opinion and commentary.


The Problem

We needed to define what Ideas would mean to readers and writers and convey it in an elegant, seamless reading experience.


Initial Research

- surveyed treatments on competitors' opinion sections
- interviewed Ideas edit team to determine what the section meant to them
- TODO: include photos


Goal

The primary goals to accomplish:
- visual distinction that's different from regular articles
- convey more nuanced depth and analysis than a normal article
- an emphasis on the author to signal a more personal reading experience
