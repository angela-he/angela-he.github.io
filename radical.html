Radical Study

A zine about Chinese American history

Frustrated by the lack of Chinese American narratives we had growing up, Samantha Keng and I collaborated on a zine about radical Chinese American history within the labor, housing rights, and racial justice movements. Researched and written by Samantha and designed by me, Radical Study ties each chapter with a character in Mandarin.


Created for any reader interested in Asian American history, we sought a friendly, digestible way to tell moments in history while also exploring an interaction with the Chinese language visually.

Feel free to email me for online access to the zine!
