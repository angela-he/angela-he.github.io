I designed a tool to allow our members to voice support for clean energy bills to their state representatives.




Goals

Community Solar is only available in a few states due to state-level policy. We had run ad-hoc email campaigns in the past to allow our members to tell their representatives to support solar legislation, and now we wanted a tool to more easily spin up campaigns whenever solar legislation arose - in short, to leverage our national customer base to push state legislators to expand solar access!

Our hypothesis was that providing customers with streamlined advocacy tools would help influence community solar policy and create additional impact.


Wireframes and user testing

Initial designs explored:

  • a module on the solar dashboard to inform customers of legislation in their state to support
  • a form for customers to learn about the specific solar bill, find their representives, and send them an email
  • success and follow-through states once a customer has voiced support by sending a message

After a few rounds of feedback from stakeholders, I conducted user tests to see how people interacted with the flow.

Final designs
