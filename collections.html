The Atlantic - Collections

A new way to create and exhibit stories

In collaboration with Caitlyn Hampton, I designed the story building tool Collections.


The Problem

Current collections of stories (channel pages, deluxe template) are stale, inflexible, and do little more than list out articles.


Initial Research

- stakeholder interviews with edit, design, sales, data, product about what a Collections tool would help with
- competitive research to see what landing pages out there were delightful and immersive to readers
- TODO: include photos


Goal

The primary goals to accomplish:
- empower editors & writers to create story collection layouts that suit their editorial needs
- reduce # of one-off page builds for product team, thus saving time & resources
- delight readers
